CS Courses Offered at East Side

East Side has began offering a comprehensive 6-12 computer science sequence in 2021. Since then, the following courses have been offered:

  • Intro to Science with Scratch
  • Intro to Cybersecurity
  • Web Development
  • Art and Coding with p5.js
  • Physical Computing with Arduinos
  • AP CS Principles

Yenmin hopes to offer more courses such as:

  • Mobile App Development
  • Game Design with Python and Unity
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • SQL

In 2022, Yenmin also founded the Girls Who Code East Side chapter.
